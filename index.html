VELAMMAL COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

We have established ourself as one of the most preferred engineering institutes in Tamil Nadu. We also has several research facilities and collaborations with universities from abroad. We offer courses in Engineering, Technology and Management. We have modern infrastructure, experienced faculty and good placements.

Department of INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY




The department aims to impart high quality education in IT and conduct top notch research in IT-related fields. We provide state-of-art infrastructure and computing facilities to students and faculty. The department has a regular computing lab as well as the state of art research labs in robotics and networks & Software development, which help the faculty and students to work on funding projects and offer consultancy services. The Department follows a well proven pedagogy which shares knowledge with the young and vibrant minds of the college.

Rules and Regulations


  • Team size is limited from 2 students(minimum) to 4 students(maximum).
  • Online Registration is compulsory.
  • Spot registration is not available.
  • Participants have to produce their college Identity Card while reporting for the competition.
  • Registration fee for each person is ₹250/-
  • Participants have to carry two hard copies of their respective Project Description.
  • Reporting Time 9:00 am on 13th April, 2023. Keep your projects ready for demo before 9:30 am.
  • Time limit for explanation of project is 10 Minutes.
  • All the participants will receive their certification of participation.
  • Last date for Registration - 08th April, 2023.
